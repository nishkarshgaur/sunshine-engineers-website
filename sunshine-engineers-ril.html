About Us

A Legacy Built on
Steel & Chemistry

In 2003, a visionary chemical engineer established Sunshine Chemicals — a gift to his life partner, a dedicated and business-minded lady. The journey began with the manufacturing and trading of industrial chemicals.

The promoters later expanded into new product lines for JRW and MIF by establishing Sunshine Engineers, focused on iron & steel structures fabrication and engineering.

Today, Sunshine Engineers & Chemicals is a renowned manufacturer, exporter, and supplier — headquartered in Ghaziabad, the National Capital Region of India. The Sunshine group is also actively involved in charity work, empowering the underprivileged section of society.

Fabrication Unit

Sunshine Engineers' Fabrication Unit specialises in the design, manufacture, and supply of iron & steel structures. Our expertise covers galvanized and non-galvanized steel fabrication for industrial, infrastructure, and energy sector applications.

Chemical Unit

Established in 2003, our Chemical Unit is the cornerstone of the Sunshine group. We manufacture and trade a wide range of high-grade industrial chemicals, with a specialisation in hot-dip galvanizing chemicals, JRW, and MIF formulations.
